Design Fundamentals Year 1

REFLECTION

Looking back on this project I can say that I have really enjoyed it, there were times where it felt like I could not get over the next hurdle, but I have managed to pull through and get something in which I am happy with. My goals were to make sure the website was functioning, to have everything required for the assignment and to use as a portfolio piece. I feel I have completed all my intended goals for the website and more, I am proud from going from no knowledge of html and css to being able to create a website that functions the way I want it to. I have added in all the tasks required into this webpage as well as including extra content from another module. I feel my website could look better however I am still happy with the overall outcome.

In the end I have ended up making a multipage website, at first, I thought I would go with a singular scrolling page as it was going to be easier. However, as I got further through the project, I felt the buttons becoming more and more of an option for me. I am happy that I changed my mind as I feel it looks much more professional, and I much prefer the way the website flows this way. I had also picked an earth colour scheme for the website, but it didn’t feel right, it didn’t feel like it was me. I went with the simplistic greyscale with a splash of light red, I felt this was right for my first website as it reflects me more.

When making the multiple pages I wanted to make sure that you can easily get back to the front page and to the other pages with ease. I have put in a navigation system on each page at the top, so it was easy for the target market to locate how to move between tabs. I have designed this to work on a computer screen, in the future I would like to push myself a bit further and make it work on multiple different platforms.

It feels very rewarding being able to see the final product and knowing it was coded by myself, sometimes it would be a struggle however I would refer to the worksheets for certain sections to try and work it out for myself. I think this is a great way to learn as I am remembering more and more things every time I look back. There were a few times where I would struggle and had to ask for help however, I tried my hardest to do it myself and I feel more accomplished being able to complete my website knowing I done the best I could.

I feel the website went well as well as the content within the website, I am happy with my designs for the posters and the typographic systems. I feel next time I could push myself more and do something a bit different with both however I do still like the end products from both tasks.

In short, I am happy with the success of this website, and I look forward to adding more to the website and/or creating new and improved websites in the future.
